She is average height. She appears to be youthful. She has wide-set sea green eyes and alabaster skin. She has shoulder length, glossy auburn hair. She has a delicate face, a classical nose and a generous mouth. She has a pair of eahnor chevrons adorned with some raven's feathers dangling from her ears.

She is in good shape.

She is wearing a filigreed eahnor choker dripping auroral emeralds, a silver-chained overgown dripping shards of glossy onyx, a sideless leather gown dyed a metallic hematite black, and some dark ora filament-strapped silk boots lofted on beveled heels.

Roelon nods once.

Aliashyrah smoothly continues, "I journeyed to Icemule a short time past in the hopes of investigating and validating the various rumors that had traversed across these lands to the halls of Ta'Illistim, my home. Armed with a mixture of curisotiy and uncertainty, I set off in pursuit of the facts."

Aliashyrah nods once.

Aliashyrah says, "Upon arriving I quickly learned that while welcoming, Icemule and her citizens dilligently guard their history and secrets against those they perceive as outsiders. With this unexpected challenge set before me, I chose to not only learn about the recent more monumental events, but to aquaint myself with some of the hisoty and traditions of this fair town and her people."

Aliashyrah honestly admits, "I cannot claim to rival some of the other fine candidates with regard to time spent in Icemule."

Aliashyrah steadily proposes, "Even so, I humbly present for your consideration that I may have something unique to offer, precisely because of the fact that I am a more recent citizen."

Sorlu looks thoughtfully at Aliashyrah.

(Aliashyrah takes a momment to scan the room, offering an occasional reserved smile to those present whom she recognizes.)

Aliashyrah diplomatically says, "The recent events in town have highlighted the strength and character of Icemule, but they have also left scattered seeds of discord and mistrust. It is only to be expected when matters of betrayal and possession are involved."

Aliashyrah says, "As someone who was not present during that tumultuous time, I possess a clean slate and an unbiased view of each citizen."

Aliashyrah solemnly promises, "Every one of you is equal in my eyes and would be treated as such."

Aliashyrah smiles.

Aliashyrah says, "I am further blessed with an outside perspective, which means that my decisions will be based solely on the current needs and desires of the town rather than muddied by past events or connections."

Aliashyrah seriously says, "The position of town councilor is one that demands a great deal of accountability and passion. Each councilor should bring to the table a unique perspective to allow the mayor the advantage of a well-rounded collection of information with which to make his decisions. This will ensure that Icemule does not become an echo chamber for recycled thoughts and actions."

Aliashyrah honestly attests, "There is still work to be done as the Abbey suffers even now with unwanted and unwelcome occupants, a murderer plaugues the otherwise peaceful streets of town, and new but somewhat tenuous alliances have been formed which will need attention."

Aliashyrah adds, "There is also a general disconnect that I have noted between the average citizen of town and those who lead. Many who I spoke to upon my arrival were oblivious to town issues and events. This is a travesty and one which I would address by encouraging open discourse and ensuring all pertinent matters are made readily accessible."

Aliashyrah humbly concludes, "I would ask that you consider me for one of the open council seats. Regardless of your decision during these elections, I will remain here working side by side with each of you to shape the future of Icemule while considering and respecting her past."

Aliashyrah demurely says, "Thank you for allowing me a chance to speak."
