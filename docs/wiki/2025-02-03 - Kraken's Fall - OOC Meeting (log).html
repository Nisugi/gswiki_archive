Kaonashi softly says, "Since I assumed my duties as Kraken's Fall Guru for Gemstone IV in the summer of 2024, I am proud to say that we have released several convenience of play features and areas designed to make the town more fun, livable, and comprehensive."

Kaonashi softly says, "At Simucon 2024, I announced the release of the Nereid's Cradle, Canopied Walk, otherwise known as the Kraken s Fall Night Market. This space was designed to serve as a community gathering point. As part of this, we expanded beach fishing across Nereid's Cradle, opened several new fishing spots on the Canopied Walk itself, and opened a brand new Brawling Pit beneath the Walk's planks. I am pleased to see that this space has received regular use, and I am thrilled that we can hold both player-run and GM-run events here."

Kaonashi softly says, "In the months that followed, we also released a Multihued Pavilion on the same Canopied Walk. This was built as an event space for meeting some lively friends, sea kobolds, but I also look forward to using it in other fun and varied ways over the coming months and years."

Kaonashi softly says, "In the spirit of convenience of play, I have also begun to work on facilities that will bring Kraken's Fall in line with other Elanthian towns and settlements, while expanding the storytelling in town. An example of this was in the newly-opened Cleric Shop attached to the Peaceful Porpoise. With this infrastructure in place, players can now more easily enjoy the recently-updated Paladin Service, as well as a few other offerings typically found in such shops."

Kaonashi softly says, "Examples of this are seen in the Atoll hunting ground, where I worked with staff from Gemstone IV's development team to eliminate bugs related to the log ride toward the Atoll and relating to the way hidden Atoll mobs interact with players and their spells. I also personally worked with players and staff to address a few OSA-related bugs and errors."

Kaonashi softly says, "All the while, I am proud to continue offering core guru services for the town, including monthly citizen GALD and WPS sessions during existing windows. I also have asked Gentleman Hoorne to continue to spread the gospel of Kraken's Fall, extolling its benefits and virtues across Elanthia. You might have seen him recently in Wehnimer's Landing, negotiating metal rights with merchants there and with Dwarves who call Teras Isle and Zul Logoth home, and generally being very brave in the face of danger."
