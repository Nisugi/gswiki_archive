Subject: A Court Session in Vornavis

This coming Feastday the 14th of Lormesta at 9:00 pm by the Empire Standard of Time, Baron Dunrith Malwind of Vornavis will be holding an open session of court. Those with ties to Vornavis are invited and encouraged to attend, and all residents of the region and friends of the barony are welcome at court. The ongoing relief effort is slated to be the main topic of conversation, however his Excellency may take petitions if time permits.

The chamberlain of the court would also like to remind all those who would attend this session of court that proper attire and behavior will be required.

(OOC: The Court session will be in two days on Saturday 1/14 at 9:00 pm EST)

Summary

Dunrith announces he's travelling to Lolle.

Maylan gives a report on donations, which Lady Kasendra and Baron Malwind recognize. Maylan gives a luxurious scent to Kasendra as a gift.

Sir Cryheart Thaxin asks to court Baron Malwind's daughter, Athalia. The Baron approves and allows it after some wrangling and Sir Cryheart presents Lady Athalia with a gift.

Lady Kayse reports on bandits on the road to the Relief Camp.

Lord Bristenn Mires offers his Order of the Azure Sun to handle the caravan trail issue.

Baron Malwind directs Lord Salnim Malwind to hold court in his stead while he's away.

Log


[Vornavis, Great Hall]
On a low dais, spanning the southern end of the hall, three regal chairs of state rest before an embroidered tapestry of the Rising Swan of Vornavis that covers the wall behind them.  Banners bearing the coats-of-arms of knights in service to the barony hang from the railing of the second floor gallery that lines the other three sides of the Great Hall.  Opposite the dais a pair of large fel doors, inlaid with gold scrolling, are set in the north wall.  You also see the Saitama disk, the Dannai disk, the Zarston disk, the Ysharra disk, the Neovik disk, the Maylan disk, the violet Salwarry disk, the Ordim disk, the Sweetsin disk, the Limorten disk, the Mynon disk, an aged chamberlain and a small side door.

You see Lady Athalia Malwind.
She appears to be a Human from Vornavis.
She appears to have come of age.  She has moss green-flecked midnight blue eyes and milky white skin.  She has very long, glossy golden brown hair pulled back into a gossamer silk snood beaded with tiny white pearls.  She has a heart-shaped face, an upturned nose and full rose-tinted lips.
She is in good shape.
She is holding a perfect winter rose adorned with silver and blue ribbons in her right hand.
She is wearing a delicate white gold diadem dangling emerald teardrops from each filigree foliate segment, a pair of opal earrings set in a platinum mounting encircled with emeralds, a silvery-sage green suede cloak emblazoned over the right breast with an ivory silk swan, an argent velvet gown with absinthe beading on the laced bodice, a pair of white lace half-gloves, and a pair of white kidskin boots secured with argent silk ribbons.

You see Lord Salnim Malwind.
He appears to be a Human from Vornavis.
He appears to have come of age.  He has thick-lashed hazel eyes and ruddy skin.  He has tied back, shoulder-length light brown hair with reddish highlights.  He has an oval face, an aquiline nose and high cheekbones.
He is in good shape.
He is wearing a thin silver coronet, a dark green cloak lined with white silk, a bright white pearl swan pendant, a brilliant white tunic embroidered with green scrollwork, a white leather belt with silver studs, a dark green leather scabbard bound in silver, some dark green trousers trimmed with white stripes, and some shiny black leather boots.

You see Gurbah Klaah, the half-ogre half-bull centaur.
His age is impossible to determine.  His long, white hair flows back into his mane, he has black eyes, and dark skin.
His lower body is covered with fine grey hair, and his hooves appear to be shod with mithril shoes.  He is wearing a dark green tabard and barding emblazoned on the chest and both flanks with a white swan rising, a heavy leather harness, some gleaming steel platemail, and some heavy leather gauntlets.

You see Baron Dunrith Malwind.
He appears to be a Half-Elf from Vornavis.
He appears to be middle-aged.  He has green eyes and fair skin.  He has receding, light brown hair.
He is in good shape.
He is wearing a golden baronial coronet adorned with emerald swans, a pristine white cloak trimmed with green velvet, a white-trimmed green silk surcoat, a gleaming emerald tunic emblazed with a white swan, a white leather belt with gold studs, a dark green leather scabbard bound in white gold, some snow white trousers with dark green striping down the legs, and a pair of bronze-buckled black boots.

You see Captain Martohck Hightower.
He appears to be a Human from Aldora.
He is taller than average and appears to be in the prime of life.  He has thick-lashed hazel eyes and tanned skin.  He has cropped, glossy chestnut hair with grey at the temples.  He has a clean-shaven face, a broad nose and broad shoulders.
He is in good shape.
He is wearing a winged veil iron helm, a thick red wool cloak lined with heavy fleece, a heavy black leather harness with a long-handled vultite spiked-hammer set with a wolf head pommel poking high overhead, a set of ornate veil iron field plate etched with a large wolf head, a pair of chain gloves with thick leather palms, a thick aged leather belt with a spiked white ora mace and a modwir-hilted feras short sword hanging from it, and a pair of heavy mail boots.

You see Lady Kasendra Chandrennin.
She appears to be a Human from Aldora.
She is taller than average and appears to be youthful.  She has gold-flecked blue eyes and fair skin.  She has long, wavy red hair twisted into a high, neat bun and held in place with some jade droplets arranged in a spiral design.  She has an aquiline nose and high cheek bones lightly dusted with pale freckles.
She is in good shape.
She is wearing a slender gold necklace of tiny jade droplets, an ivory satin gown embroidered with a silvery feathered pattern, a pair of ivory satin gloves, a tiny gold ring, and a pair of pale green silk slippers.

Maylan works her fingers under her death mask and scratches her head.

Maylan says, "Ah yes."

Speaking to Kasendra, Maylan says, "We wish to present you with a gift."

Maylan offers Kasendra a frosted glass bottle with an arctic blue stopper.

Speaking to Kasendra, Maylan says, "This is a scent known only to our fine house. May you think of us when the winds of winter blow your way."

Maylan fidgets.

Kasendra says, "Thank you."

Cryheart says, "I respectfully request to court your daughter, Lady Athalia."

Speaking to Cryheart, Dunrith says, "We have long heard tale of the many brave deeds and actions you have taken on behalf of Vornavis, the Landing, and innocent people."

Speaking to Cryheart, Dunrith says, "But we must admit that taking such a step here before this Court, with these many people present certainly does stand out as a brave deed."

Cryheart says, "She gives me the courage."

Goldstr agrees with Dunrith.

Cryheart says, "Your Excellency."

Athalia blushes a delicate shade of pale pink.

Dunrith says, "She has ever been a source of inspiration for great things."

Kasendra smiles.

Cryheart says, "Aye your Excellency."

Cryheart says, "I recall the first task you gave me to display my chivarly towards your daughter, a long time ago."

Cryheart says, "The attraction has grown greatly."

Cryheart says, "With respect."

Cryheart bows.

Cryheart smiles at Athalia.

Cryheart bows to an aged chamberlain.

(Cryheart steps back.)

An aged chamberlain seems to mutter a few things to himself.

An aged chamberlain says, "The next petition before the Court is one Kayse Thaellian.  She has a matter of importance for His Excellency."

(Kayse takes a few confident steps to the front of the crowd, she gives a short respectful nod to the group sitting on the dais, before steading her gaze.)

Kayse formally introduces, "My name is Kayse Thaellian--I suppose I am best known for how I spin a tale, as well as being an officer within the House of the Arcane."

Kayse simply continues, "I have been on a few supply runs to the relief camp.  One of the times a tree had fallen in our path--this was before the heavier snow.  I suppose it could have fallen on its own...but I had a feeling we were being watched as we cleared the area."

Kayse carefully mentions, "This most recent trip, on the way back a few days ago, the wagon came to a sudden halt.  More debris in the path--but this time, rocks, branches...it appeared man made."

Dunrith gazes with interest at Kayse.

Kasendra frowns.

Kayse slowly says, "When we finished clearing the road, a crossbow bolt shattered against the side of the wagon and a voice rang out..."

Kasendra gasps.

Kayse folds her wings across her back.

Salnim turns an inquisitive ear toward Kayse.

Kayse quotes, "Ya all have a nice safe trip home now.  It be dangerous of here.  Best be watching where you stray."

Kayse cautiously notes, "I am not sure if Duvernith was able to send word in time, but we believe they weren't wishing to rob the wagon, but were looking for a person."

Kasendra says, "That is terrible."

Kasendra frowns.

Speaking to Dunrith, Kayse says, "For future supply runs, I think we should be on our highest alert--but most importantly anyone of importance heading in or out of the camp."

Dunrith says, "We were made aware of this, however the details of the report were few.  Thus we were hoping to hear from one present during this incident for ourselves."

Kayse nods understandingly at Dunrith.

Dunrith says, "For that we thank you for bringing this matter out into the open."

Kasendra nods at Martohck.

Dunrith says, "We are in agreement in urging caution."

Kayse politely says, "I am glad it was made aware to you.  Thank you for your audience.  I hope whoever is responsible can be caught before something happens besides a road block."

Dunrith says, "An incident like this does make us wonder who or what might be responsible, and furthermore why."
Kayse nods in agreement at Dunrith.

Kasendra adopts an agreeable expression.

Dunrith says, "However we appear to be lacking in this information at this time.  Thus our steps forward must be in caution."

Bekke asks, "Pardon me. If I may offer a .. thought?"

Speaking to Dunrith, Kayse offers, "All we can do moving forward is to exercise caution and hope we learn more."

Kayse glances over her shoulder at Bekke.

Speaking to Salnim, Dunrith says, "We recommend Captain Gurbah and you liase with Lady Kasendra and Captain Martohck to investigate this incident and take the necessary precautions."

Salnim nods at Dunrith.

Kasendra nods.

(Kayse's eyes shift across each member on the dais, observing every detail she can.)

Dunrith says, "We are dealing with sovereign territories and thus cannot simply dispatch the Guard and be done with it."

Salnim says, "We will see what can be found out, father."

Kasendra agrees with Salnim.

Kasendra says, "We will."

Dunrith nods.

Kayse nods once at Dunrith.

An aged chamberlain says, "Thank you for your petition before the Court."

(Kayse turns abruptly back towards the crowd and makes her way back to her spot.)

An aged chamberlain says, "Our next petition before the Court is one Sir Bristenn Mires, Knight of the Empire and member of the Order of the Azure Suns."

Bristenn confidently strides a few steps forward.

Bristenn bows low, abasing himself before all.

Bristenn formally observes, "My lords, my ladies, captains."

Speaking to Bristenn, Dunrith says, "We are ever gladdened to have you here before, Sir Mires."

Bristenn clearly replies, "It is my honor, my liege, to serve this fair barony and her noble house."

Bristenn slowly begins, "However, what I bring to this court is less of glad tidings- it, in fact, hinged 'pon the witnesses of the incidents along the caravan trail."

Dunrith gazes with interest at Bristenn.

Bristenn confidently offers, "Given that these are sovreign terri'tries and provincial borders, and that the Guard cannot be dispatched, I submit the Order of the Azure Sun to task on this troubling news."

Bristenn evenly adds, "We are few in number, my liege, yet I am confident we can bring about a resolution without the situation escalating moreso."

Bristenn respectfully assures, "We are at your leave, sire, and that of your house, and will do our mortal finest."

Bristenn bows respectfully, his eyes fixed firmly on the ground.

Speaking to Bristenn, Dunrith says, "We also would berift to mention that dispatching you on our behalf when we could not dispatch our own men, still can result in issues of sovereignty.  To that end we know that you will, as ever, step carefully in this matter."

Mynon nods reassuringly.

Bristenn sincerely agrees, "Rather, your Excellency."

Salnim says, "I am sure we can find a use for Sir Mires, father."

Salnim nods at Bristenn.

Bristenn nods gravely, clasping his hands behind his back.

Bristenn clearly concedes, "That is all I had for this honorable court, save my humblest thanks for but a moment of the floor."

Athalia smiles at Bristenn.

Dirra smiles proudly.

An aged chamberlain says, "The Court thanks you for your honorable petition, Sir Mires."

Bristenn inclines his head.

Bristenn bows respectfully, his eyes fixed firmly on the ground.

Bristenn takes a few steps back.

An aged chamberlain says, "That was the final petition we had for the Court, Your Excellency."

Dunrith inclines his head.

Dunrith nods to the aged chamberlain.

Dunrith says, "Thank you Lord Chamberlain."

Dunrith says, "Before we end this session of Court, we have one more thing to make mention."

Dunrith says, "In light of this recent news, we believe it is in the best interests of this Court, and the Relief Effort, to hold a court session again in the future to update on this matter."

You adopt an agreeable expression.

Dunrith says, "While we will be away visiting Lolle, we hearby instruct Lord Salnim Malwind to hold Court in our stead."

Salnim nods at Dunrith.

Speaking to Dunrith, Salnim says, "I will try to not burn the Hall down while you are gone, father."

Dunrith shakes his head.
