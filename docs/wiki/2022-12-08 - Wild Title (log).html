2022-12-08-Wild Title (log)

Log captured from the view of Opalina


Nalver says, speaking to you, "I would like to officially sign up to be considered as a delegate."

You ask, speaking quietly to Nalver, "Would you like to subit anything for the council to read?"

Brokkrsten asks, "Ya want me ta raise ye?"

The ghostly voice of Kelfur says, "Aye please."

Nalver says, "If you need my title, I am Lord Nalver of Icemule, Duke of Danjirland, Major Commander General of the Grenadiers of the Knuckleheads, and Siege Infantry of the Drakes Vanguard."

Brokkrsten exclaims, speaking to Kelfur, "Alright, up ya comes!"

Nalver says, speaking to you, "I have also met royalty of the nations and have good relations with many from Vaalor. There are many there that would vouch for me and being even tempereed, fair, and just."

Brokkrsten says, speaking to Kelfur, "There ye go! All better."

Kelfur says, "Thank you."

Brokkrsten asks, speaking to Kelfur, "Ye want spirituals?"

Kelfur says, "Aye if you could."

Brokkrsten says, "Most kind of yas."

Nalver says, speaking to you, "I have met Gasen, who is Mustyr's Squire. I even gave him a gift of a ice blue gem which he loved."

Kelfur says, "Thank you all."

You quietly say, "That could be very helpful."

Kelfur says, "Now to the inn to rest a bit."

Nalver says, speaking to you, "Gasen is the squire who has been tasked with relations between Illistim and Ta'Vaalor."

Nalver says, speaking to you, "Most importantly, I can speak Elven."

Nalver says, "Court is held in Elven. Always."

Nalver says, "Anyone not speaking Elven will need a translator. That could ruffle feathers."

You ask, speaking quietly to Nalver, "I am curious, You know we can only choose two candidates, But you have valuable knowledge and skills. If for some reason you were not picked as a deligate this time.. Would you be willing to help share your knowlege with the Deligates going?"

You ask, speaking quietly to Nalver, "Possibly even help connect your friend to the group for support in ettiquite?"

Nalver says, speaking to you, "That seems like you are telling me that I won't be considered as a delegate."

Nalver says, speaking to you, "But I'll do whatever is best for this town."

You say, speaking quietly to Nalver, "I can not guareentee you would be choosen no. I can't promise that to anyone."

You quietly say, "I don't know who will be the final two chosen by the entire council."

You quietly say, "Will be."

You quietly say, "Sadly."

You say, speaking quietly to Nalver, "But I think you Love Icemule and would Greatly help the town, and who ever does get chosen if it may be yourself if you would share your knowledge."

Nalver says, speaking to you, "I imagine that a parlay will have to be made. Sorlu's choice in exchange for the choice of the remaining three or four."

Dendum exclaims, in Gnomish, something you don't understand.

Nalver says, "Rumors about on that topic."

Nalver says, "Parley, even."

Dendum asks, "Is this where we sign up to be emmisary of icemule to the King?"

You quietly say, "I am sorry I can not speak of those matters."

You quietly ask, "Were you interested in signing up for the deligation?"

Dendum says, "No was just wondering if this was spot."

Dendum says, "Have spoken to that king before...is reasonable elf...will be fine whoever is sent as long as they do not declare the wars."

You quietly ask, "Well maybe?"

Nalver says, "Apologies. Had to step away a moment."

Nalver says, "Thank you for hearing me."

You say, speaking quietly to Dendum, "I agree."

Nalver says, "I must get back to doing things."

You quietly say, "Thank you Nalver."
