Bejeweled Blades

[Bejeweled Blades - 8031003]
Along the walls of this rustic wagon, fine dwarven craftsmanship is displayed in the form of expertly forged weapons. A large Ragnak Clan crest takes up most of the wall opposite the wagon's entrance, wrought in relief upon a slab of polished rainbow glaes. Three vultite racks are affixed to one side, each bearing a symbol of the elements. You also see a gem-encrusted sign.
Obvious exits: out 
a gem-encrusted sign

In the Common language, it reads:
Weapons on the RED vultite rack can flare with bursts of flame.
Weapons on the BLUE vultite rack can flare with an intense cold.
Weapons on the GOLDEN vultite rack can flare with intense vibrations.

On the red vultite rack you see: a ruby-hilted dwarven vultite warsword, a ruby-studded dwarven vultite limb-cleaver, a ruby-hilted dwarven vultite khopesh, a ruby-hilted dwarven vultite gladius, and a ruby-hilted dwarven vultite parrying dagger.
