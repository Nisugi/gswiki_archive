104 potion script
Jump to navigation Jump to search

This script is looped to keep creating pale sky-blue potions. Only having as much water as you need for the task will limit it from making additional potions. Press ESC to exit a script. In this script the cauldron is set as cauldron, and the alchemy container is a satchel. The ghoul nails are bundled.

A pale sky-blue potion
  1. Add water
  2. Add white hook mushroom
  3. Boil
  4. Add 2 doses of ghoul nail from lesser ghouls
  5. Simmer
  6. Add powdered star sapphire
  7. Chant Disease Resistance (104)
potion:

put light caul
waitfor You focus on the
put get water in my satch
waitfor You remove a
put water in cauldron
put flask in my satchel
put get my white mushroom
waitfor You remove
put put my mushroom in cauldron
put get my nail
waitfor You remove
put bund remove
put put my nail in cauldron
waitfor You place
put bund remove
put put my nail in cauldron
waitfor You place
put put my nail in my satchel
put alch simm
waitfor bringing the contents to a slow simmer.
pause 1
put get my star sapp
waitfor You remove put put my sapp in caul
put alch chant 104
waitfor Flickering wisps of essence coalesce over the solution as your chant continues, swirling in rhythm with the sound of your voice, and lazily drift down to vanish into the solution.
pause 1
put get my empty flask
waitfor You remove an empty flask
put alch seal
pause 1
put put my pot in my satch
waitfor You put a crystalline flask containing a pale
pause 1
goto potion
