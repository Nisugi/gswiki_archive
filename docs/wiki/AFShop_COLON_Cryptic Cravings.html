Cryptic Cravings, Food Stall

[Cryptic Cravings, Food Stall - 8031014]
Well-built and compact, the haon food stall is purposely neutral in hue, allowing its wares to shine and attract. Placed in various baskets and on multiple trays are treats ranging from simple sweet snacks to savory morsels to revitalizing beverages. A long menu board has been placed at one side of the broad, tiered countertop. Colorfully striped, a thick oilcloth canopy provides some measure of protection from wind and other weather elements.
Obvious exits: out
