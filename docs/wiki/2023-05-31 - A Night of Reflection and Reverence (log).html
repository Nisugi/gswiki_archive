Speaking amusedly to Sorlu, Adene asks, "Is that what you call it these days?"

Adene grins slowly.

Alosaka rubs his chin thoughtfully.

Adene flicks at the end of the pale green jade half-ring in her lip thoughtfully with her tongue.

Imrys smiles at Sorlu.

Adalfuns says, "It's always entertaining."

Speaking to Adene, Sorlu says, "A Councillor's work is never done."

Imrys inclines her head.

Sorlu flashes a quick grin.

Sorlu says, "Pleasant evening, all."

Sorlu offers a polite nod, touching two fingers against his forehead in a brief salute as he tucks his opposite hand into the bloodjewel-fastened pocket of his obsidian waistcoat.
