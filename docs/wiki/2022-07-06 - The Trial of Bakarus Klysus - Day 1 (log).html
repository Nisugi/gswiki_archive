Arianiss says, "An idea I had for the use of the Crimson Legion, Silver Gryphons, and Town Guard."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Did you tell this idea to anyone else at any other time?"

Arianiss says, "No."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Was anyone immediately in your vicinity when this was discussed?"

Arianiss says, "Not that I could detect."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Was this conversation spoken aloud?"

Arianiss says, "It was not."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "After this discussion, was a note found containing the details of the idea you shared with Bakarus?"

Arianiss says, "Yes."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "How long after this conversation took place?"

Arianiss says, "Based on the better recollection of Aethyra, the next evening."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Who was the note addressed to?"

Arianiss says, "I don't remember.  Zeban or Arnabas.. but it was a pristine version of the one Reiphe shared with us at the town meeting."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Was the note signed?"

Arianiss says, "Yes."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "What was the signature?"

Arianiss says, "A stylized B."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Has Bakarus ever been possessed in your presence?"

Arianiss says, "To my non-expert opinion, yes."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "When this happened, did his manner or appearance change from the norm?"

Arianiss says, "To my perception and awareness, just his voice."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "How did his voice change?"

Arianiss says, "Didn't sound like him.. I don't remember the specifics.  Whatever it was, was attempting to be thought of as Zeban."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd asks, "Have you ever heard Bakarus' voice to change similarly since?"

Arianiss says, "No."

Zeminar darkly says, "He isn't possessed anymore..."

Speaking to Arianiss, Perigourd says, "Thank you sir, that is all I had."

Arianiss says, "You're welcome."

Zeminar darkly says, "We shall cross examine."

Arianiss says, "Yes."

Speaking darkly to Arianiss, Zeminar says, "The case resides on wether or not Mr. Klysus was possessed."

Arianiss says, "Okay."

Speaking darkly to Arianiss, Zeminar asks, "Which you testified that indeed he was, correct?"

Zeminar darkly asks, "You testified to this?"

Arianiss says, "It appeared to me that he was during the meeting, yes."

Zeminar darkly says, "Yes of course."

Zeminar darkly says, "I have a parchment here, that was also found."

Arianiss adds, "The town meeting, not the drill session."

Zeminar darkly says, "In a dark alley in this here town.  Among the excrements and fluids."

Zeminar darkly says, "I shall read this parchment."

Arianiss says, "Okay."

Zeminar darkly says, "In the common language, of course."

Zeminar darkly says, "Counselor Gespry."

Perigourd says, "Objection, the defense is presenting evidence which hasn't been proven to be relevant to the questioning of the witness."

Speaking darkly to Teaberry, Zeminar says, "This is quite relevant."

Perigourd asks, "How are we to know how the witness can testify to anything regarding it when no connection to Councilman Arianiss' knowledge has been made?"

Zeminar darkly says, "I am proving a point, your honor."

Teaberry asks, "What is the point, counselor?"

Zeminar darkly says, "These letters...  This B...  It has no legal weight within a courtroom."

Zeminar darkly says, "We have a posessed man.  Being set up for slaughter."

Zeminar darkly says, "The court has clearly seen he was possessed."

Teaberry says, "It's getting late and there is much yet to discover. I declare the court in recess until the evening of Feastday the 9th, 9:30 elven time."

Teaberry says, "I look forward to seeing you again in three days."

Rammael says, "I will let everyone know what delayed Mayor Dabbings."

Rammael says, "...once I have found that out myself."
