Recap of 2023 Meeting and Project Requests:

Completed Requests:

  • Boatswain at the Frostmain
  • Nation of the North Named
  • Evaluated town defenses
  • Make krolvin and half-krolvin more visible in town
  • Attract more merchants to IMT
  • Polish up the Magical Burrow
  • Fix up rooms across town
  • Repurpose Ancient Burrow for the half-krolvin
  • Add coffee to IMT

Still to Come:

  • Icemule Temple revamp
  • School of Magic in the Magical Burrow
  • Create a government for Northwatch
  • Improve gambling at Silvermule
  • Fix the Rat Races
  • Yarn store
  • Signposts and watchtowers outside of town
  • Add tea
  • Evaluate alternative travel to Crystalhall
  • Hinterwilds fishing

Requested at Meeting:

  • Improvements to IMT hunting grounds and trails so that players do not need to travel through high-level grounds to get to lower ones
  • Cooking proteins for sale in IMT (butcher shop)
  • Fishing in IMT
  • Check out Pinefar porch to see what the area's safe zone is
  • Improve Stout Street Armory (general access storage, darts, etc.)
  • Exception to mount script for Town Center script

Teased:

  • Continuation of the Icemule Trace ongoing
  • Consequences of a nation's rapid growth - showing some strain at the seams
  • Some surprises coming soon
  • Resurgence of the Dusk Coven as a potential foe
