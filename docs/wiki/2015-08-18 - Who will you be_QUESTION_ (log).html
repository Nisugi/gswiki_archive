2015-08-18 - Who will you be? (log)
Jump to navigation Jump to search

This log is from 8/18/2015 from the viewpoint of Cryheart (prime).

Summary

Glissando made an appearance just before Frontier Days. 

Title: "Who will you be?"

Glissando sings pleadingly:
"I only wish that you would listen
To a conscience that knows you so well
It tells you in a voice so loud
Nothing comes from indifference
And only failure rewards the proud
Why won't you look deep inside yourself?"

"Will you find some kind of conviction?
Will you bid the hero farewell?
Will you finally find your own salvation?
Or will you be defined by things that could have been?
Maybe you aren't the hero
But if you don't try, how will you know?
I guess time will have to tell
I guess time will have to tell"

"You curse your birth
With every ragged breath
Feeling you have no worth
You've longed for death
It's blinded you for way too long
Damn it all, why won't you make a difference from now on?
Cause you're wide awake to it all
Yeah, you're wide awake to it all"

"Before tomorrow comes
Before it's way too late
Don't turn and walk away
Take the hand in need
Before tomorrow comes
Before it's way too late
You could change everything
Tell me, who will you be?"

"Does anyone care
That it ain't right what you're doing?
Does anyone share
All the things that you're knowing?
Does anyone dare
Justify how you're living?
Does anyone here care at all?"

"Before tomorrow comes
Before it's way too late
Don't turn and walk away
Take the hand in need
Before tomorrow comes
Before it's way too late
You could change everything
Tell me, who will you be?

"You could be so much more than you are
You should be so much more than you are
Why won't you be so much more than you are?
Oh! This you have to see!
You! Undivided, are so much stronger!
So tell me, who will you be?"
