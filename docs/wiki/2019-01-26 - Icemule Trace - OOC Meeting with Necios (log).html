Necios says, "I also hope that people will be willing to post on the forums so Newsby will catch happenings, as well as update the wiki in brief."

Necios says, "I can't do all the things, I'm so sorry."

Necios says, "TownCrier reminds me of the days I need to merchant like 80% of the time."

Necios recites:

    "The meeting is officially over as it was only scheduled until 11.  Feel free to stay or leave."

Necios points at Jalodg.

A big ugly kobold guard leans toward the door, ears raised, with a suspicious look on his face.

Jalodg melodically says, "One question in two awkward chunks. It's not Icemule specific, no worries if there's not enough time to answer right now."

Jalodg melodically asks, "I'm glad that you mentioned WARN and other mechanisms to deal with harassment. Could you outline a good example/procedure on how someone playing a mischievous character can pull another player/character that would potentially cause them pickpocketing, theft, harm or death?"

Jalodg melodically asks, "Could the 'offending' pickpocket, ambush or take some negative action first and see if the other player wants to go along with it? Should the 'offending' player reach out to the other player first before having their mischievous character engage (which might take away from the surprise and excitement of the RP and mechanical stakes)?"

Speaking to Jalodg, Necios says, "Okay, so pickpocketing is a permissible mechanic.  If you SEE someone pickpocket you or someone else, you can ACCUSE them at the deputy's office or in front of the deputy."

Necios says, "Otherwise, close your containers and keep your pockets empty of silvers."

Jalodg nods at Necios.

Necios says, "So that's not a violation of policy."

Necios says, "As far as attacking goes, the CHALLENGE verb is your solution."

Necios says, "You can challenge someone to first blood or first death."

Necios says, "Killing or hurting someone in town has its own repercussions."

Necios points at Rongolak.

Rongolak says, "I just had a request to add Voaris and Laethe more to the town, since Oleani is the patron arkati of halflings, it seems fitting and would be nice to see them included as lesser spirits to the town, maybe a statue with the three of them with halflings cavorting about."

Rongolak says, "That's all."

Necios says, "I like that.  I'm not sure they could be altars to those deities unless they were outside of the town because of the temple having so many deities."

Rongolak nods.

Rongolak says, "Bring the love back is all I'm askin."

Necios says, "Maybe ICICLE could be involved with that.  It's on my list now."

Necios points at Rucer.

Rucer says, "I had to stop out earlier, so apologies if I missed any question or discussion regarding this already. I wanted to also request a perm sanctuary in TC, not only to prevent non-consentual PvP in a resting spot, but also to prevent scarabs from latching onto everyone in the room, if a box should go awry. Of course, said sanctuary can and should go away during storytime."

Speaking to Rucer, Necios says, "A permanent sanctuary has been requested and declined in town center for decades."

Rucer nods understandingly at Necios.

Necios says, "It has been turned down each time as well."

Necios says, "Part of the reason for this is honoring the original town designer's wishes."

A big ugly kobold guard leans toward the door, ears raised, with a suspicious look on his face.

Necios says, "Another part of this is... not every town center should be a permasanct."

Necios shrugs.

Necios exclaims, "A third part is DEMONS!"

Necios says, "We allow demons in the open, and with a permasanct, they couldn't hang ou t there."

Fuil asks, "If it was permasanct, would that do something about all the smooching that goes on in TC?"

Necios says, "It would increase the smooching."

Speaking to Necios, Rucer says, "Alright, wasn't the answer I'm looking for, but I'll continue to use the tools I have available to prevent non-consnetual PvP. Thank you."

Necios says, "I've put it on my list, but I am uncertain as to whether that request will be re-evaluated."

Rucer smiles at Necios.

Necios nods at Rucer.

Necios smiles.

Necios points at all the hierarchies.

Necios grins.

Necios says, "But it's on my growing list."

Necios points at Sabotage.

Sabotage asks, "Do you have an idea how long this chapter of the storyline is going to be?  Any plans for arkati involvement in it, say all that banging waking up Koar or something?"

Necios says, "I invite communes to any appropriate deity."

Necios says, "I don't know if those deities will reply."
>
Necios says, "I know a well done ceremony will invite a reply."
>
Necios says, "This chapter will be over on or around March 31."

Necios says, "But this chapter will involve more exploration, puzzles, a fair bit of slaying, and a few final revelations."

Sabotage asks, "Is this the final chapter?"

Necios says, "This is Daukhera's final chapter."
>
Necios says, "There are elements that will continue to the next part."

Nodyre says, "We'll probably see Thurfel again."

Necios grins.

A big ugly kobold guard gazes warily out a nearby porthole for a time.

Necios says, "I do not have any plans to resurrect Thurfel."

Speaking to Sabotage, Necios asks, "Was that your final question?"

Sabotage nods.
>
Sabotage says, "Thanks for your time tonight."

Necios asks, "Great.  That's all that's on the list... does anyone have anything further?"

Speaking to Bellaja, Necios says, "No, though I will announce BIG nights in advance."

Bellaja nods at Necios.

Speaking to Bellaja, Necios says, "My time frame is usually 9 or 10 eastern starting on a weekend night."

Bellaja nods at Necios.

Speaking to Necios, Nodyre asks, "Any plans for the rift or Pinefar this year?"

Speaking to Nodyre, Necios asks, "What kind of plans?"

Speaking to Necios, Nodyre asks, "Enhancements / storylines?"

Speaking to Necios, Nodyre says, "Let me rephrase."

Speaking to Necios, Nodyre asks, "Is ICICLE able to coordinate improvements to Pinefar?"

Nodyre says, "More like getting an herb box."

Nodyre says, "Instead of the dang cupboard."

Nodyre says, "Having the fire always lit."

Speaking to Nodyre, Necios says, "Enhancements of the critter type are team Dev's responsibility.  Storylines are possible, or portions of storylines."

Speaking to Nodyre, Necios says, "As far as enhancements to the Pinefar area, like building extensions or whatnot, those would likely not be approved."
