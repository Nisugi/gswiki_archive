December 22, 2024

From the TownCrier team, hosted on the TownCrier ESP channel. On December 22, 2024, Wyrom released the State of Elanthia at Jara's YouTube Channel. You will want to see that video (probably) before reviewing the followup from the Q&A below. This log was sharing a live Discord voice chat with other players who could only be in-game.

Luxelle summarized the questions, Leafiara summarized Wyrom's answers (and sometimes another GM).

Lead-In

Leafiara: "Starting up with some Platinum questions because there were a lot of them."


A Wide Variety of Platinum-Related Questions

Luxelle: "Q: Platinum boxes capacity?"
[Platinum's going to have boxes into which people put their items right before migrating to Prime.]
Leafiara: "Answer: aiming for item limit."



Luxelle: "Q: Can platinum characters not use migrated items?"
Leafiara: "Answer: Migrated Plat items are account attuned and marked as Plat items, so it's essentially making sure that only Plat characters can use Plat items."



Luxelle: "Q: Platinum silvers will transfer over?  And locker expansions?"
Leafiara: "Answer: locker expansions will come over, though there are limits that will still be intact."
Leafiara: "I think I missed the one on silvers, if anyone heard it."
Rovvigen: "Silvers will transfer over."



Luxelle: "Question: Is the same race/prof to merge only for existing characters or also for merging into level 0?"
Leafiara: "Answer: make the character on Prime be the race/profession you want to be because that's what it'll be."



Luxelle: "Question: What happens to the Plat Instance?"
Leafiara: "They expect to do a couple waves of transferring people from Platinum to Prime in January, then a break in February, then more in March."
Leafiara: "Hasn't been decided how long they'll continue to do migrations, but things like pay events pose an obstacle because they don't want many more items created in Plat before migrations are finished."
[Elaborating now after the fact, Wyrom brought up the possibility that migrations could even be throughout 2025. He's easing into it because of any bugs or issues that could pop up with the first wave.]



Luxelle: "Question: Are there plans to provide unattuning services on Plat transferred items once in Prime?"
Leafiara: "No unattuning Plat items."



Luxelle: "Question: Lumnis brooch in both instances - what will happen?"
Leafiara: "'Item collisions' are expected to happen and will have to be worked out on a one-on-one basis. (Other examples include temporal weapons or Sorrow.) Wyrom will work with people individually to figure out a solution."
Leafiara: "But for the specific example of Lumnis brooches, he thinks they might be able to combine them with an ASSIST."



Luxelle: "Question: What are the plans for the Plat instance after Migration."
Leafiara: "Players can stay in the legacy Platinum instance if they want, making it what they want to be, but there'll be no GM support. If it ever got shut down fully, there would be lots of advance notice."



Luxelle: "Question: Wouldn't it be [easier] to transfer Plat characters and all their things without doing all these things?"
Leafiara: "It would be easier, but it's not as feasible as they'd like and they wanted to have tools like the ones they've made just in case they needed to be able to move characters over in the future."
[That's what I said live. Clarifying, the idea was that in case they had to fully shut Plat down one day due to lack of activity, they needed tools to expedite the process, so they might as well have them now.]



Luxelle: "Question: Family Vaults and Locker expansions from Plat?"
[We didn't catch the answer live, but Rovvigen got it from others, so representing that here:]
[TownCrier] Rovvigen: "regarding family vaults and locker expansion for plat migration - it would happen, but there was limitations behind it."

While they don't police lnet per se, they do monitor it since it still happens in-game [in] a sense. They've heard the recent suggestion of blocking whispers and will be looking into more tools along those lines. Wyrom does recommend using Discord blocking tools, likewise. Also important to let staff know. Sometimes records or logs can be pulled, though it takes a very long while."
Leafiara: "There have also been some updates to policy have been pending for five years, going back to David Whatley. They were trying to mirror Dragonrealms, but the game communities and situations are a bit different, so they won't be doing that anymore. Wyrom got approval to make a new policy, including some new numbers, and is partway through it."
